Reading has been something I’ve loved my whole life, but I’m just getting back into it. I haven’t read much since middle school, especially after the COVID lockdowns kept me from visiting my school library. By the time I was back, I’d lost the habit and forgot how much I enjoyed it. For years, I’ve mostly just read the books assigned to me. But this summer, I decided to pick it up again, so I created this page on my website to keep track of all the books I’m reading.
